About Ore Horizon

Ore Horizon is a 2D mining game where you dig for valuable ores, sell them, and upgrade your mining skills and gear. Explore vast underground worlds and find rare minerals to become the ultimate miner!

Game Features

  • Explore randomly generated underground caves
  • Collect over 5 different types of ores
  • Sell ores to buy better gear
  • Upgrade your mining skills to dig deeper and faster
  • Unlock rare tools and secrets hidden underground
